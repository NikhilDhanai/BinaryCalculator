Quick and easy online Binary Calculator


Instructions

[0] ,[1] - Number Keys

[ToDecimal] - Convert Binary to Decimal

[ToHexa] - Convert Binary to Hexadecimal

[ToOctal] - Convert Binary to Octal

[+],[-],[X],[/] - Operations

[<<], [>>] - Shift operator

[C] - clear screen

[DEL] - Remove Last Character

[=] - Equal to
